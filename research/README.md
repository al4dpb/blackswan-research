# Research

The core archive — long-form research articles on consciousness, organized by subject pillar.

Each pillar has a `README.md` introducing its scope. Each article folder contains:
- `en.mdx` — English version
- `es.mdx` — Spanish version
- `meta.json` — title, sources, evidence tier, publication date
- `social.json` — short-form summaries (where present)

## Pillars

- **`scientists/`** — researchers whose work documented consciousness phenomena
- **`institutions/`** — labs, universities, and research programs
- **`government-programs/`** — declassified state-funded consciousness research (Stargate, MKUltra, Soviet psi, Gateway Process)
- **`phenomena/`** — psi, ganzfeld, global consciousness, manifestation, anomalous cognition
- **`practitioners/`** — modern method-developers (Wim Hof, Joe Dispenza, Stan Grof, etc.)
- **`ancient-wisdom/`** — traditions and lineages with documented effects
- **`mental-work/`** — interventions for sleep, attention, anxiety, focus
